FIDELITY HOTEL is still a WIP fangame of Pizza Tower!
The story follows shortly after the true ending of Pizza Tower, with Pizzahead being shot into space.

[[Keep in mind, this fangame runs off it's own custom engine it may not be entirely accurate to the real Pizza Tower!]]

***THE SAFETY OF YOU IS PRIORITIZED!! I ADVISE YOU TO BE DISCREET, DO NOT SHOW ANYTHING OF THIS FANGAME FOR YOUR SAKE (Unless you're _itvaries)***

What I'm currently looking for:
	- Any possible bugs with the OYH input system
		- Such as certain parts of controller support not working, or anything faulty with keyboard input
	- Any possible crashes or unintentional behavior/bugs
		- You'll know what unintentional behavior is once you notice it
	- Any personal nitpicks or suggestions you may have
		- This fangame isn't for the masses, it's for us, I am catering this game toward YOU
	- Any performance issues in X part of game
		- Do keep in mind there is very little optimization currently!

CURRENT AREAS:
	- Waybackyard, Complete visuals and layout.
	- Tutorial, layout subject to change, placeholder visuals
	- Rock Bottom, unfinished layout, subject to change, no visuals
	- Hub, layout subject to change, placeholder visuals
